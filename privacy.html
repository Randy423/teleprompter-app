Privacy Policy

Last updated: April 20, 2026  ·  Effective immediately

The short version: PromptFlow runs entirely in your browser. We do not collect, store, or transmit your scripts, recordings, or personal data to any server. Everything stays on your device.

1. Who we are

PromptFlow ("we", "our", or "us") is a browser-based teleprompter application. Our registered address is available upon request by emailing us at privacy@promptflow.app.

2. What data we collect

We collect the absolute minimum necessary to operate the service:

  • Scripts you write or paste — stored locally in your browser's localStorage. Never transmitted to our servers.
  • Video recordings — stored temporarily in your browser's memory (RAM) during your session. Never uploaded anywhere. Deleted when you close the tab.
  • Microphone audio — accessed only when you use Voice Control. Audio is analysed in real time in your browser to detect speech volume. No audio is recorded, stored, or transmitted.
  • Premium trial date — if you start the Voice Control free trial, the start date is stored in your browser's localStorage to calculate remaining trial days. This data never leaves your device.

3. What data we do not collect

  • We do not collect your name, email address, or any identifying information unless you voluntarily create an account.
  • We do not use analytics, tracking pixels, or advertising cookies.
  • We do not sell, rent, or share your data with third parties.
  • We do not store your recordings on our servers — they exist only in your browser memory until you download or discard them.

4. Camera and microphone access

PromptFlow requests access to your camera and microphone solely to power the teleprompter recording feature. This access is:

  • Requested in real time — the browser will show a permission prompt.
  • Used only while you are actively recording on the display screen.
  • Terminated immediately when you leave the display screen or close the tab.

We never access your camera or microphone in the background.

5. Account data (if you create an account)

If you choose to create a PromptFlow account, we collect your email address and a hashed password for authentication purposes only. We do not store payment card details — payments are processed by Stripe, whose privacy policy applies to payment transactions.

6. Cookies and local storage

We use browser localStorage to remember your saved scripts and trial status between sessions. We do not use third-party cookies or tracking cookies of any kind. You can clear this data at any time through your browser settings.

7. Data retention

Script data in localStorage persists until you delete it within the app or clear your browser data. Session recordings exist only in memory and are permanently gone when you close the tab or discard them.

8. Your rights

Under GDPR and applicable privacy laws, you have the right to:

  • Access the personal data we hold about you
  • Request correction or deletion of your data
  • Object to or restrict our processing of your data
  • Data portability — receive your data in a machine-readable format

To exercise any of these rights, contact us at privacy@promptflow.app. We will respond within 30 days.

9. Security

Because all sensitive data (scripts, recordings) is processed entirely on your device, there is no server-side risk of a data breach affecting your content. All web traffic to PromptFlow is served over HTTPS.

10. Children's privacy

PromptFlow is not directed at children under 13. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children. If you believe a child has provided personal information through our service, please contact us immediately.

11. Changes to this policy

We may update this policy occasionally. We will notify registered users by email and update the "Last updated" date above. Continued use of PromptFlow after changes constitutes acceptance of the updated policy.

12. Contact us

For privacy questions or concerns: privacy@promptflow.app
